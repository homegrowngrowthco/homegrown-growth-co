Common Questions

Frequently asked.

HubSpot or Salesforce — which is right for us?

It depends on your motion, team size, and budget. HubSpot is typically faster to implement and easier for smaller teams with less complex processes. Salesforce is more powerful for complex sales motions, multiple product lines, or enterprise requirements. We help you make that call based on your actual situation, not vendor marketing.

How long does a CRM implementation take?

A structured implementation typically takes 4–8 weeks depending on complexity, data volume, and how much cleanup is needed. Migrations from one CRM to another generally run 6–10 weeks. We scope it precisely after the discovery phase.

What happens to our existing data?

Nothing gets deleted until we've confirmed the migrated data is clean and complete. The migration protocol includes a full backup, field-by-field mapping, deduplication, and a validation pass before anything goes live.
