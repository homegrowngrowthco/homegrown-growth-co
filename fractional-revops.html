Common Questions

Frequently asked.

What's the difference between fractional RevOps and a full-time hire?

A full-time VP of RevOps costs $150–200K+ base, takes 3–6 months to hire, and is typically overkill until you're well past $10M ARR. Fractional gives you the same senior thinking and execution at 20–30% of the cost, with a clear exit when you're ready to bring it in-house.

How much time does fractional RevOps actually require from me?

Minimal. The point is to take work off your plate, not add to it. Expect a 30-minute weekly sync and occasional async questions. Everything else is handled.

What does a typical engagement look like month to month?

Month one is a structured discovery and roadmap. From there: weekly standups, async execution on the ops backlog, regular reporting health checks, and a monthly review of priorities. Everything is documented so nothing is a black box.
